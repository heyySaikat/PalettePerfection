Objective:
Mission is to refresh the website until all five boxes are the same color. It sounds simple, but it's not.
How to Play:
  1. Refresh the Page: Each time you refresh the page, the boxes will change to random colors.
  2. Check the Colors: After each refresh, observe the five boxes and see if they all match in color(there are only 5 color codes possible).
  3. Repeat: Keep refreshing the page until you get all five boxes to be the same color.
